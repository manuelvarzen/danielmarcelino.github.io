

This week, the Brazilian Finance Minister delivered a brief talk where  he summarizes some details relating to a new bill that will improve great deal our understanding about wealth and income distribution in Brazil. 
The report series will start next year, but in his talk, he already presented very interesting results, though addressing tax returns data for the year of 2014. 
This is a huge step forward because home surveys as PNAD, usually underestimate the affluence among the rich and super-rich; therefore, this information will complement the existing analysis about income distribution with other statistics.
